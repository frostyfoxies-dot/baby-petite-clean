# Scope
This policy applies to:
- kidspetite.com
- www.kidspetite.com
- api.kidspetite.com

# Out of Scope
The following are not in scope for our security program:
- Social engineering attacks
- Physical security
- Denial of service attacks
- Issues in third-party services

# Disclosure Timeline
- We aim to acknowledge reports within 48 hours
- We aim to provide a timeline for resolution within 7 days
- We request that you give us reasonable time to fix issues before public disclosure

# Bug Bounty
We do not currently have a bug bounty program, but we appreciate responsible disclosure of security issues.
